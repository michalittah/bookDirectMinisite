Hotel Description
Near central Ottawa, Ontario attractions such as Parliament Hill, this all-suite hotel offers spacious accommodation with comfortable amenities as well as contemporary facilities. Parliament Hill is 1 km away.
Albert at Bay Suite Hotel features one-bedroom suites with fully equipped kitchens, spacious work areas and closet space. Guests can relax on the balcony or use the in-suite free wireless high-speed internet access.
After exploring the area, guests can workout in the state-of-the-art fitness centre with cardiovascular equipment.
Rideau Canal is 1.7 km from this property. Byward Market is 7 minutes' drive away.
This property is also rated for the best value in Ottawa! Guests are getting more for their money when compared to other properties in this city.
