Hotel Description
Relax in a sophisticated Tel Aviv hotel styled as a beach house. Enjoy the cool breeze and beautiful sunsets in the heart of the city, overlooking the Tel Aviv beach.
The Shalom Hotel & Relax offers 51 rooms designed to pamper guests that want to enjoy the resort atmosphere of Tel Aviv, Israel.
Some rooms (type - Spa) feature attractive sitting areas; others feature bathrooms with a view of the beach, while unique romantic spa rooms welcome guests with an indulgent hot tub facing the magnificent view.

All guests are welcome to enjoy the amazing rooftop lounge - the perfect spot for an afternoon of relaxation
Location
With a stay at Shalom Hotel & Relax, Tel Aviv - an Atlas Boutique Hotel, you'll be centrally located in Tel Aviv, steps from Independence Park and minutes from Hilton Beach. This hotel is close to Gordon Beach and Old Tel Aviv Port.
